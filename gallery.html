MY CURRENT PROJECTS

Real work, real results. A visual showcase of my electrical and automation projects.

18,300 SQ FT INDUSTRIAL HALL

This industrial hall was fully planned and wired by me, including load calculations, distribution design and complete installation. The project included three-phase systems, high-load circuits for machinery, cable-tray routing, lighting layout and planning for future expansions. All work executed to commercial standards for reliability, safety and long-term durability.

EXTERIOR LIGHTING & POWER

The exterior was equipped with a complete lighting and power system designed and installed by me. Scope included weatherproof LED fixtures, protected cable routing, outdoor distribution points and integration of all exterior loads into the central electrical concept. Built for safe visibility and reliable operation in harsh conditions.

HIGH-BAY LIGHTING SYSTEM

The high-bay lighting system was completely planned and installed by me. Layout includes energy-efficient LED fixtures, uniform spacing for consistent light distribution and dedicated circuits sized for industrial loads. All wiring routed along the steel structure with clean, service-friendly design supporting long operating hours and future expansion.

TRANSFORMER STATION

I executed the complete low-voltage side installation, including high-capacity feeders, NH fuse switch units, busbar systems and metering equipment. The station provides stable transition from medium voltage to the building's internal distribution. All components dimensioned for heavy industrial loads with safe maintenance access.

HIGH-CAPACITY FEEDER UNITS

This section shows the 300 mm² feeder units I installed, corresponding roughly to 600-700 kcmil in US sizing. Used for heavy industrial service entrances and high-load distribution. Installation includes mechanically secured lugs, protected cable routing and modular enclosure system designed for safe access under continuous high current.

DISTRIBUTION WALL

Complete low-voltage circuit system for the facility. I installed and organized all outgoing circuits on modular distribution units, including lighting, machinery feeds, socket circuits and dedicated equipment lines. Cabling routed cleanly over cable trays, bundled for load groups and prepared for clear identification and maintenance.

CEE OUTLET PANELS

One of the CEE outlet panels I installed throughout the facility. The unit provides both 230V and three-phase 400V connections for machinery, tools and temporary equipment. All outlets protected inside a compact enclosure with integrated circuit breakers, mounted on steel structure for stability and easy access.

SMART CONTROL SYSTEM

Custom smart control system I developed and installed. Left display shows the ESP-based weather station monitoring wind and rain conditions for automatic window control. Right display provides lighting control for all zones. Built on ESP microcontrollers, touch displays and custom web-app backend, integrating modern IoT into an industrial environment.

RESIDENTIAL PV STORAGE

Complete residential PV storage system I installed with Solax hybrid inverter and modular high-voltage battery unit. Installation includes clean cable routing, DC and AC protection, communication wiring and full integration into the home's electrical system. Provides energy storage, backup capability and optimized self-consumption.

EV WALLBOX INSTALLATION

Outdoor Huawei EV wallbox I installed, mounted on concrete boundary panel and wired through protected conduit for durability. Fully integrated into the property's electrical system, providing safe and reliable AC charging for residential use. Weatherproof, discreet and positioned for convenient vehicle access.

STRUCTURED NETWORK CABINET

Structured network cabinet I installed with organized patch panel, Cat-cables, coax distribution and managed Ethernet switch. All connections routed cleanly with defined cable paths for stability, easy troubleshooting and long-term reliability. Provides solid backbone for high-speed data, IPTV and internet services.

VERTICAL CABLE ROUTING

Vertical cable routing along the steel structure of the industrial hall. Cables for sectional gate, control units and auxiliary systems guided in protected conduits and cable trays. Designed for durability, easy maintenance and future expandability while maintaining clean, organized appearance on the structural framework.
