01

18,300 SQ FT INDUSTRIAL WELDING HALL

Designed and installed the complete electrical system for a full-scale industrial welding facility. The project included a 400A three-phase main distribution, heavy-duty feeder cables up to 600 kcmil (approx. 300 mm²), high-load circuits for welders, compressors and industrial machinery, and multiple CEE16 and CEE32 industrial power outlets, described for U.S. readers as European 230V/400V three-phase industrial connectors similar to NEMA L15-20 and L15-30 systems. Work included structured cable-tray routing, precise load balancing, selective protection coordination and fully documented distribution planning. Delivered to a zero-defect standard with clean, safe and reliable installation.
